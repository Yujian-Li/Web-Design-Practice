DIY Your Weekly Healthy Meal Plan

Step 1: For each day of a week, pick one meat dish and one vegetable dish.

Step 2: Pick 2-3 snacks for weekends.

Step 3: Pick as much soup or porridge as you like, schedule to any days of a week. Please do not keep soup or porridge longer than two days even in the fridge.
